Each phase transition requires evidence. You can't move from human-led to AI-led on faith — you need to demonstrate that AI-generated recommendations match human-quality decisions at a sufficient rate, across a sufficient range of conditions.  
  
This is where **AIP Evals** becomes structurally important. Evals provide the evidence base for expanding AI autonomy. Before each phase transition, teams run comprehensive evaluation suites that measure AI performance against defined criteria. As workflows mature, continuous monitoring and benchmarking ensure that performance doesn't degrade.  
  
The combination of progressive automation as the strategy and Evals as the evidence mechanism creates a principled, defensible path from human-led operations to increasingly autonomous workflows — at whatever pace is appropriate for each specific use case.
