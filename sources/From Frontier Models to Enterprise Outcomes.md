A frontier model can generate text, reason about code, and analyze images. But an enterprise needs that model to understand its specific business context, access its proprietary data, respect its governance policies, integrate with its existing systems, and take actions that affect real-world operations. The gap between what frontier models provide and what enterprises need is enormous. Foundry and AIP bridge that gap.  
  
If a frontier model is the brain of your Enterprise Agents, **Ontology is its body**.
