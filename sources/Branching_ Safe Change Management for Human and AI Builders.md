Finally, Foundry and AIP are platforms for builders.  
  
When both humans and AI agents are building on the same platform, you need a mechanism that prevents changes from breaking production until they've been reviewed. This is what **Foundry's global branching** model provides, allowing for safe human + AI teaming in the builder world.  
  
This isn't just version control. It's a governance mechanism — runtime controls govern what agents and users *can do* in production, branching governs what changes *make it to* production in the first place.
