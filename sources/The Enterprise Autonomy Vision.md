The ambition behind Foundry and AIP is not to do what's minimally viable — it's to do what's maximally ambitious. The vision is an enterprise where thousands of AI agents wield thousands of specific tools across every workflow imaginable — not as a far-off aspiration, but as a concrete engineering target that shapes how the platform is built today.  
  
Foundry and AIP are already deployed in some of the most demanding operational contexts in the world: staff scheduling in hospitals, underwriting workflows in insurance, build operations in shipyards, and defense and intelligence missions across the spectrum. The stakes are real, the data is messy, and the margin for error is thin.  
  
This is what Palantir's forward-deployed approach to product development means in practice: working backwards from the hardest problems customers face, and letting those problems define what needs to be built. Above all else, it's a radical orientation around the outcome.
